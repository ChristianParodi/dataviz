Distribution of CO₂ Emissions by Region and Country (2022)

An important factor to consider is how much each region influences the global emissions and, respectively, which country of a specific region is the main responsable. In the following visualization, it's trivial to spot the world top emitter (i.e. China), as it outrace all the other countries by at least 5 billion of tonnes of CO2 emissions

Design choices

  • For each continent, only the top 2 emittors are shown, while all the other countries are grouped in the "Others" category.
  • The "Fossil" and "Land" categories are placed in the center of the diagram, in order to show the contribution of each country and continent to the overall emissions.
  • Each country color-coded using the same color of the continent it belongs to.
